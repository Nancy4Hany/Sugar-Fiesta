WHO WE ARE..

Sugar Fiesta is a dessert store that sells desserts that are low in calories and sugar-free,
primarily catering to those who have illnesses like diabetes that call for them to follow a
healthy diet plan, as well as anyone who generally follows a diet plan and, of course, those
who have a sweet tooth.
